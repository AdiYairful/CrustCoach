1.Pizza Dough:

Ingredients: 600 mil of cold water, 10 grams of fresh yeast, 850 grams of white flour, 10 grams of salt and 20 grams of sugar.

Step 1: Add ALL the flour to the food proccessor's bowl.

Step 2: Add ALL the sugar & salt into the mix.

Step 3: Add ALL the yeast.

Step 4: Proccess the mixture until everything is dispersed evenly.

Step 5: Keep proccessing while dripping all of the water SLOWLY into the mixture.
